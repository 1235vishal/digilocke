What is DigiLocker?

DigiLocker is your personal document folder in the clouds. Launched under the Digital India programme, DigiLocker is an initiative of the Ministry of Electronics and IT. It is a cloud-based platform designed to store citizens’ digital documents. The stored documents can also be shared and verified through DigiLocker.

Aadhaar cards, COVID-19 vaccine certificates, mark sheets, driving licenses, ration cards and vehicle registration certificates are some of the common documents saved by citizens in DigiLocker.

Adding documents to DigiLocker

Apart from uploading your documents to DigiLocker, you can also add issued documents, such as a passport, PAN card, driving license, etc., to DigiLocker.

  • Passport
  • PAN card
  • Driving license
  • Aadhaar card

In the documents upload section of the Passport Seva website, you will find a self-declaration page with the option - Grant DigiLocker Access. Using this, you can allow Passport Seva to access your DigiLocker. On the next screen, you must select the “Fetch from DigiLocker” option.

In the DigiLocker dashboard, select the “Search Documents” option. Search for PAN, and the “PAN verification record - Income Tax Department” option appears. Enter your personal details and click the “Get Documents” box. Your PAN will appear in the “Issued Documents” section of DigiLocker.

In the DigiLocker dashboard, click on the “Documents you might need” section and select Driving License. Click on “Get Document”, and the driving license will appear in the “Issued Documents” section.

The DigiLocker dashboard has a dialogue box that says “Get your e-Aadhaar”. Click on it, and you will receive an OTP on your Aadhaar-registered mobile number. Upon verification, you will be redirected to the “Issued Documents” page, where you will find your e-Aadhaar.

Frequently Asked Questions

DigiLocker is a digital document storage and sharing platform by the Indian government. It allows users to store, access, and share official documents and certificates digitally. Users receive 1GB of storage space linked to their Aadhaar number. Documents can be accessed through the DigiLocker website or mobile app, and users can also link their DigiLocker account to various government services for seamless access to documents and digital signatures. It simplifies document management, reduces paperwork, and enhances convenience for citizens and government agencies.

Yes, DigiLocker employs robust security measures, including Aadhaar-based authentication and OTP verification, ensuring the confidentiality and integrity of stored documents, making it secure for sensitive information.

Yes, you can access DigiLocker without an Aadhaar card. While Aadhaar-based authentication provides certain benefits, alternative methods like mobile number-based registration are available for access.

Yes, eSignatures in DigiLocker are legally valid in India. They comply with the Information Technology Act, 2000, making electronically signed documents legally enforceable in various transactions and processes.

To link DigiLocker with government services, log in to your DigiLocker account, navigate to "Issued Documents," and use the "Share" option to share your documents directly with government agencies requesting them.

No, DigiLocker services are free for individuals. The government offers this platform without any charges for storing, accessing, and sharing digital documents and certificates.
